Legal Structure — SEC-Compliant Investment Framework

Each RRN investment opportunity is structured as a separate Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV LLC) that owns the underlying assets and issues tokens to investors. This structure keeps investment activity legally separate from the ministry's charitable operations — protecting both investors and the mission. RRN LLC serves as the program operator and earns a management fee. All formal investment activity requires SEC compliance review before any offering launches.
